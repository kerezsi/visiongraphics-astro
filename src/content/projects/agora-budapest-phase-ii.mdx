Developed by HB Reavis, with architectural contributions from MAKE Architects and Finta Studio.

Features a mix of office spaces, commercial services (including grocery stores, restaurants, cafes, gym, and more), and green areas.

Designed to be a sustainable and modern urban hub, enhancing the quality of life for both workers and local communities.
