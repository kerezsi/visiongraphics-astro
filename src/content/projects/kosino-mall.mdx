<ProjectTasks>

We created comprehensive exterior visualizations for the Kosino Mall project, including aerial views, night renderings, detailed facade treatments, and a site plan. Our renderings highlighted the distinctive origami-inspired folded roof surfaces that served as the unifying architectural element across the development. The visualizations showcased how the design worked with the natural landscape, particularly emphasizing the integration of the existing water reservoir as a feature element with surrounding decks and pathways.

<ProjectStory heading="The Story:">

The design cleverly incorporated an existing water reservoir, transforming what could have been a disadvantage into a unique waterside development with restaurants and cafes accessing the water via wooden decks. The project aimed to provide not just a shopping destination but a comprehensive lifestyle center with recreation spaces and family-friendly areas.

</ProjectStory>
