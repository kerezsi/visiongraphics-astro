<Lang code="en">
Our team at Vision Graphics was responsible for creating comprehensive 3D visualizations of both exterior and interior spaces of the Somlói út residential complex. We developed detailed marketing floorplans showing the 36-147 m² apartments across the three buildings, highlighting the spacious layouts and functional design. Our visualizations also showcased the underground parking facilities and the 7639 m² plot's landscaping, helping potential buyers envision the complete living experience this premium development offers.

<ProjectStory heading="The Story:">

<Lang code="en">
The Somlói út 64-68 is the location of an exclusive residential complex in Budapest, Hungary, designed by Zoboki-Demeter and Associates Architectural Office (ZDA). This high-end development, known as the Somlói Lakópark (Somlói Residential Park), is situated on the southwestern side of Gellért Hill, one of the most prestigious areas in Budapest.
</Lang>

<Lang code="hu">
A Somlói út 64-68 egy exkluzív budapesti lakókomplexum helye, amelyet a Zoboki-Demeter és Társai Építészirodája (ZDA) tervezett. Ez a prémium kategóriás fejlesztés — a Somlói Lakópark — a Gellért-hegy délnyugati oldalán helyezkedik el, Budapest egyik legrangosabb területén.
</Lang>

</ProjectStory>
