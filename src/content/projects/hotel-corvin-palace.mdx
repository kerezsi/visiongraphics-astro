The comprehensive visualization package covered the full breadth of the property's interior spaces: guest room variations in classical and modern styles, circular room configurations, lobby, wellness areas with pools, thermal bath facilities, conference venue, hamam, medical treatment areas, restaurant, bowling facilities, and cave bath installations.
