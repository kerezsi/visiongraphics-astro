title: "DA! Mall"
year: 2013
location: "Moscow, Russia"
designer: "Dyer Group"
description: "DA! Mall is a 115,000 sqm lifestyle center that balances shopping convenience with a unique identity. The mall features a dramatic central volume with spacious interiors, abundant natural daylight, and carefully selected materials that create an exceptional family shopping experience."
story: |
  The architectural concept revolves around a unique triangular floor plan layout called "the heart," which serves as the main component of both the architectural and retail concept. This central two-level space is enclosed by a two-level gallery and food court, with bridges providing access to cinemas and entertainment areas. The main atrium sits at the focal point of the building, featuring an elegant roof light structure that floods the interiors with natural light, creating a bright and spacious atmosphere.
tasks: |
  We created detailed 3D visualizations for the DA! Mall project, working closely with the architects to bring their vision to life. Our team developed both exterior and interior renderings that showcase the mall's unique triangular layout, dramatic central volume, and elegant roof light structure. These visualizations helped communicate the architectural concept effectively to stakeholders, highlighting the natural light flooding through the atrium and the distinctive zones throughout the mall. Our work focused purely on the visual representation of the architectural design, providing the architects with compelling imagery to present their innovative retail concept.
