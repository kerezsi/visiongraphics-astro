Vision Graphics Kft. created photorealistic 3D visualizations for the Officium Offices project, including exterior renderings, interior spaces, sectional views, and photo integrations that helped bring the architects' vision to life. We produced a comprehensive 3D animation that showcased the building's design features, spatial relationships, and integration with the surrounding environment, helping stakeholders visualize the final result before construction was completed.
