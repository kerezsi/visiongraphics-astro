designer: "Dyer Group"
description: "The OZ Bowling center was part of a 5,000+ sqm entertainment unit that included multiple feature zones such as a sports bar, karaoke bar, and VIP bowling area."
story: |
  Following the success of the OZ Krasnodar Shopping Mall, Dyer was commissioned to design interiors for various tenants and special entertainment units owned by the landlord. The OZ Bowling project was designed in parallel with the OZ movie theatre, with both spaces connected by a grand lobby that served as both a link and a separator between the two entertainment areas.
tasks: |
  Our team created interior visualizations for the OZ Bowling project, showcasing the diverse yet cohesive design across the various functional zones. The renderings highlighted:
  
  The distinctive look of each area (sports bar, karaoke bar, VIP bowling, etc.) while maintaining overall design continuity
  
  The grand lobby connecting the bowling center and movie theatre
  
  The use of lighting, materials, and custom furniture to create unique atmospheres in each zone
  
  How the design facilitated smooth transitions between different functional areas
  
  These visualizations helped communicate the complex, multi-faceted nature of the entertainment center's interior design, demonstrating how each space would function individually while contributing to a cohesive overall experience.
