description: "Sasad Liget 6 represents the final phase of the highly successful Sasad Liget residential development in Budapest. story: |
  Following the remarkable success of previous phases, Sasad Liget 6 completes this ambitious residential project with 6 buildings housing 186 apartments and 6 retail spaces designed to serve residents' daily needs. The development's excellent location provides quick access to downtown Budapest via the M4 metro line or buses 8, 53, 139, and 239, while drivers can reach the M1-M7 highway junction in just 5 minutes.
tasks: |
  For the Sasad Liget 6 project, Vision Graphics was commissioned to create comprehensive visual content showcasing the development's appeal. We produced detailed exterior renderings highlighting the modern architecture and abundant green spaces, designed immersive interior visualizations for three typical apartment layouts, and developed interactive 360° virtual tours allowing potential buyers to experience the spaces virtually before construction completion. Our visualizations helped Biggeorge effectively market these premium properties during the crucial 2018 sales period when the project was nearing completion.
