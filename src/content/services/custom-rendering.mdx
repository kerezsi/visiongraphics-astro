Most render farms take your files and process them as-is. We take your files, prepare them properly, then render them — which means faster frames, fewer errors, and lower cost. Fifteen years of V-Ray expertise means we catch the problems before they become failed overnight renders.

## What we support

- **3ds Max + V-Ray** — all recent versions, including legacy scene conversion
- **V-Ray Standalone** — VRscene submission, distributed rendering
- **Blender** — Cycles and EEVEE
- **Rush priority** — same-day turnaround for urgent deadline jobs

We look at your scene before submitting. Heavy geometry, missing textures, wrong GI settings, oversized texture maps — all of these turn a 6-hour render job into a 36-hour one. We find them first and fix them with you.

Common issues we resolve before rendering:
- V-Ray artifacts, GI flickering, and exposure problems
- Render-time crashes caused by memory overflows in complex geometry
- Missing UVs and texture reference problems
- Light cache issues specific to camera path animations
- Proxy geometry failures that only appear at render time
