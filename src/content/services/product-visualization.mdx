Photo shoots happen after production. By then your design is locked, your marketing timeline is squeezed, and every variant — colour, material, size — means another shoot. We flip the sequence: final-quality imagery from your CAD files, ready before the first unit ships, with every variant at the same cost as the first.

<YoutubeEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aiQ8tUT2xFk" title="Product animation — feature demonstration sample" />

## What you get

- Hero shots for e-commerce, advertising, and print — any angle, any background
- Colour and material variants without re-shoots
- Feature animations showing how the product works
- Exploded views for technical documentation and pitches
- Interactive 3D configurators for web — buyers pick their own spec
- Campaign materials delivered before production begins

An interactive 3D configurator turns your product range into a self-service sales tool. Buyers pick colour, material, and options; they see the result immediately in photorealistic quality — on your website, on any device. No separate renders per variant. One model, infinite combinations.

<YoutubeEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SZDejBVAV_c" title="Product animation — feature demonstration sample" />

This works particularly well for furniture, fixtures, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment with multiple configuration options.

<YoutubeEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w3FA-emHwUA" title="Product animation — feature demonstration sample" />
