**Headline:** Turn Plans into Decisions.
**Format:** Full-bleed split composition, landscape 1920×1080

**Left half — the plan:**
- Dark technical drawing paper texture (`#0f0f0f` with slight grain)
- Dense architectural plan lines in cold white/blue (`#c8c8c8`, thin 0.5px)
- Showing a building floor plan with dimensions, axis lines, north arrow
- Slightly overexposed at the cut edge, lines fading into light

- Photorealistic exterior render of the same building, golden-hour afternoon light
- Glass facade catching warm orange reflections, sharp shadows
- Mature trees in foreground, slightly out of focus (depth of field feel)
- Real-feeling photography — not "CGI-looking"
- Dark vignette at the far right and top edges

**The cut itself:**
- A thin red line (`#da1313`, 2px) marks the exact boundary between plan and render

- At the cut, lines from the plan seem to "resolve" into the geometry of the render

**Text — minimal, integrated:**
- Top-left over the plan half, in `#8a8a8a` small caps, light weight:
  `ARCHITECTURAL PLANS`

## Infographic E — Process as Architecture
**Headline:** From Brief to Delivery
**Format:** Isometric process visualization, square or landscape

### Visual composition
The 7-step process is shown as an isometric building cross-section — each step occupies a "floor" or "layer" of a building being assembled from bottom to top. The building starts as a bare foundation at the bottom and is complete at the top.

**Layers (bottom to top):**
1. **Foundation** — labeled `BRIEF` — raw concrete, plans laid flat on it
2. **Structure** — labeled `3D MODEL` — bare wireframe geometry visible on the floor
3. **Envelope** — labeled `LIGHTING & MATERIALS` — facade being applied, warm light emerging from windows
4. **Occupied interiors** — labeled `TEST RENDERS` — furniture, people visible through cut section
5. **Finishing** — labeled `FINAL RENDER` — polished materials, sharp edges
6. **Landscaping** — labeled `POST-PRODUCTION` — trees, paths, surroundings added
7. **Delivered** — labeled `DELIVERY` — full scene, golden-hour atmosphere

**Feedback arc:**
A red curved arrow on the right side of the isometric view loops from layer 4 back down to layer 2. Labeled: `Revision round`

**Color treatment:**
- Lower layers: cool, desaturated, technical-feeling
- Upper layers: warm, golden, photorealistic
- The transition from technical to photorealistic happens gradually as the building is assembled
- Red accent only on labels, the feedback arrow, and the floor number badges

**Text:**
- Step labels float to the right of each layer, connected by a thin rule
- Main title top-left: `FROM BRIEF TO DELIVERY` in `#f0f0f1` 700
