# Infographic: Product Visualization

**Tagline:** Market before you manufacture.

---

## Infographic A — The Flip
**Format:** Hard diagonal split, landscape 1920×1080

**Left half:** CAD technical drawing — orthographic product views (top/front/side), dimension lines, tolerance callouts, cold engineering aesthetic, near-black background with steel-grey lines.

**Right half:** Photorealistic hero product render of the same object — studio lighting, sharp specular highlights, floating on neutral dark background. Could be a designer chair, industrial fixture, or consumer device — something with clear material character.

**The cut:** red diagonal line along a product edge — the split follows the object's silhouette, so the geometry is shared across both halves.

**Text:** Bottom center spanning the cut — `MARKET BEFORE YOU MANUFACTURE.` white 700. Small below: `Vision Graphics · Product Visualization`

## Infographic B — Variant Explosion
**Format:** Radial composition, square or landscape

**Center:** One hero product render — clean, lit, precise.

**Radiating outward:** 6–8 variants of the same product arranged in a arc — different colourways, surface finishes, material options. Each variant slightly smaller, same lighting. Thin red lines connect center to each variant like spokes.

**Bottom text:** `ONE MODEL.` red 700 · `EVERY VARIANT.` white 700
**Small:** `No re-shoots. No extra cost per colour.`

## Infographic C — Before the First Unit Ships
**Format:** Split timeline, landscape

**Visual:** A horizontal red timeline line. Left end: a 3D wireframe/CAD model. Right end: a product still life photograph (or photorealistic equivalent) in a marketing layout — lifestyle scene, copy, branding. Above the timeline at the midpoint: a calendar icon showing the campaign launch date. Below: the product manufacturing timeline — dotted, labelled "first unit ships" at a point well to the right of the campaign launch.

**Key tension:** the campaign assets exist to the left of "first unit ships."

**Headline:** `CAMPAIGN-READY BEFORE PRODUCTION BEGINS.`
